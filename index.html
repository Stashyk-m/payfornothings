Pay for Nothing.

An art performance  ·  ongoing

This is an art performance about the nature of consumption.

We live in a world that has perfected the act of selling — where desire is manufactured, value is perceived, and the distance between wanting and buying has been reduced to a single tap.

Pay for Nothing removes the product entirely. What remains is the transaction itself: the impulse, the decision, the receipt. The ritual of purchase, stripped of its justification.

People pay. They receive nothing. An AI — itself a symbol of our technological moment — writes them a personal note acknowledging what just happened.

We are as surprised as you are.
